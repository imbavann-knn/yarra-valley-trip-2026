Before You Pack

Good to Know

A few things that'll make the trip run smoother

✦ ✦ ✦
🌡️
Winter Weather

Expect 6–13°C in Yarra Valley. Lake Mountain will be below zero on snow day. Pack:
◆ Thermals & base layers
◆ Waterproof jackets
◆ Gloves, scarves, beanies
◆ Warm boots for the kids

🚗
Getting Around

Self-drive the entire Yarra stay — no public transport in the valley. You'll need 2 cars minimum for 8 pax. GPS works well. Most roads are sealed. Lake Mountain road may need snow chains — check conditions day before.

👶
Kids First

60–70% of the itinerary is built around the kids. Healesville Sanctuary — kids FREE during school holidays. Most wineries are genuinely family-friendly. Pack snacks, change of clothes for the snow day.

💰
Budget Guide

Farmstay: A$975–700/night (depends on pick)
Healesville Sanctuary: ~A$57/adult, kids free
Lake Mountain: Entry + toboggan hire
Restaurants: A$60–120pp for dinners
Chocolaterie: Free entry ✓

📶
Connectivity

Phone coverage is patchy in the valley and nonexistent at Lake Mountain. Download offline Google Maps before you leave Melbourne. Most farmstays have good WiFi. Healesville town has full coverage.

🛒
Stock Up First

Healesville town has a Coles and a good IGA. Stock up on groceries on Day 1 before heading to the farmstay. Meals at the house are half the fun — budget a few nights of big group dinners.
